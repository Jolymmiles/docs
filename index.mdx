Remnawave Telegram Shop is a Telegram bot for selling and managing VPN subscriptions. Use this documentation to deploy the bot, configure payment systems, and keep operations reliable.

<Note>
  You can pair Remnawave Telegram Shop with **Remnawave Panel** to manage nodes, users, and subscription links.
</Note>
